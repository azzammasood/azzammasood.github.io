KiryanaAI started from a simple observation at a nearby kiryana store: the shopkeeper was trying to manage rush-hour sales, credit entries, payment calculations, and customer requests with a phone calculator and a small notepad.

That moment shaped the core question behind our Google AI Seekho Hackathon Pakistan 2026 project:

**What if every dukandaar had a digital munshi powered by AI?**

KiryanaAI is a voice-first business advisor built for Pakistani shopkeepers. Instead of typing into complicated apps, vendors can speak naturally in Urdu, Roman Urdu, or English to log sales and expenses, receive smart business insights, and get actionable recommendations powered by AI.

## My Work

I worked on the backend architecture and final integration of all services, connecting the voice, insights, and frontend pieces into a working MVP.
