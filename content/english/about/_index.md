## Intro

My name is Ahmad Uzzam Masood.

I am a Data Engineer with a deep interest in building systems that provide high-quality, consistent information for downstream use cases, whether that is analytics, machine learning, or AI research.

I enjoy building data products, but I also care about the deeper parts of the Data Engineering Lifecycle: security, data management, DataOps, data architecture, orchestration, and software engineering.

Most recently, I worked as a Principal Data Engineer at Nayatel until June 2026.

## Interests

Beyond the Data Engineering Lifecycle, I read books about technology and I am currently reading O'Reilly's *Fundamentals of Data Engineering*. On Substack, I follow Data Gibberish and Data Engineering Central among others to stay current with practical data engineering ideas and industry discussions.
